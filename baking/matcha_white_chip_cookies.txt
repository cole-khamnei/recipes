Ingredients (metric):
	- 256 g all purpose flour
	- 1 tsp baking soda
	- 0.5 tsp salt
	- 2 tbsp matcha powder, see note 3

	- 227 g unsalted butter
	- 100 g granulated sugar
	- 213 g light brown sugar
	- 1 tsp vanilla extract
	- 2 eggs, room temperature

	- 170 g white chocolate chips

Ingredients (imperial):
	- 2 cups + 2 tbsp all purpose flour
	- 1 tsp baking soda
	- ½ tsp salt
	- 2 tbsp matcha powder, see note 3

	- 1 cup unsalted butter
	- ½ cup granulated sugar
	- 1 cup light brown sugar
	- 1 tsp vanilla extract
	- 2 eggs, room temperature

	- 1 cup white chocolate chips


Instructions:

1. Brown butter over medium heat until golden yellow, move to heat proof bowl
2. Mix flour (2 cups + 2 tbsp), baking soda (1 tsp), salt (1/2 tsp), matcha (2 tbsp) in dry bowl
3. Mix white + brown sugar into browned butter, add eggs + vanilla and whisk (~1 min)
4. Add dry to wet slowly, mixing with spatula until dough forms
5. Mix in white choco chips
6. rest in fridge for >= 60 mins (cover to reduce moisture loss)
7. Make dough balls (~3 tbsp) on baking sheet, cook at 350 degrees, bake for 10-12 minutes
8. Let cool for 10 minutes and then move to cooling rack for 15 mins extra cooling
