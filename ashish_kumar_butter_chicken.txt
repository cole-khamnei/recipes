tomato paste:
- 500g tomatoes ***
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp kashmiri chili powder
- 2 tbsp veg oil


1. cut tomatoes, puree in food processor
2. strain tomato puree into pot
3. add sugar, kashmiri chili powder, and veg oil to pot
4. boil on high, reduce to medium and cook until reduced to half
