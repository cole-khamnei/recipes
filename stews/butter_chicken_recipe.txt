process:

gravy:
2-3 large onions sliced
4 large tomatoes cut in half
3-4 garlic pods whole
1 inch ginger sliced
1 tbsp chili powder (deggi mirch)
5-6 cloves
1 inch cinnamon stick
3 bay leaves
5-6 black peppercorns
4 tbsp butter
2 green cardamom
salt to taste

add all to pot, cook a little dont let get color
add water (2-3 cups?)

cook about 15 mins then blend
strain it, if you really want, or remove bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cloves

butter chicken:
2 tbsp butter
1 tsbp chili powder
1 tsp coriander

melt butter then add spices until aromatic
add gravy to pan

3 tbsp cream
1 tsp honey	
chicken

1-2 drops of kewra water
1 tbsp toasted fenugreek leaves
