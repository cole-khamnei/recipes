stew ingredients
	wets / fresh:
		- 2 tins of chickpeas (800g), drained (480g)
		- 11 garlic cloves, peeled,10 left whole and 1 crushed
		- 30g  ginger julienned
		- 400g cherry tomatoes (or datterini)
		- 3 red chilis, lengthwise split, spice variable
		- 1 tbsp tomato paste
		- 200ml olive oil
	
	dry / spices:
		- 2 tsp cumin seeds, roughly crushed in mortar + pestle
		- 2 tsp coriander seeds, roughly crushed in mortar + pestle
		- 1⁄2 tsp ground turmeric
		- 1⁄2 tsp chili flakes
		- 2 tsp kashmiri chili powder
		- 1 tsp caster sugar
		- 1 tsp salt

yogurt sauce ingredients:
	- 180g Greek-style yoghurt
	- 1 garlic clove crushed
	- 15g picked mint leaves
	- 30g fresh coriander, roughly chopped
	- 1 tbsp lime juice + lime wedges for serving
	- salt to taste


instructions:
    1. mix chickpeas, garlic cloves, ginger, tomatoes, chilis, tomato paste, spices, sugar, salt, and oil in sauté pan/pot with lid
    2. cover and cook in over at 300°F for 75 minutes, stirring occassionally
    3. put yoghurt, mint, fresh coriander, lime juice, crushed garlic and 1/4 teaspoon of salt into a food processor and blitz until smooth
    4. Serve with yoghurt and lime wedges
